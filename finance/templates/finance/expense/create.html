Expense Record Helper

This form records general expense entries and generates payment vouchers.

Field Explanations:
  • Category: Pick the category for this expense.
  • Payment Method: How the payment was made (cash, transfer, dollar pay, etc.).
  • Reference: Transaction reference or voucher number (auto-generated if blank).
  • In Favour Of: Person or organization receiving the payment.
  • Description: Brief description of the expense.
  • Line Items: Break down expense into multiple items. Total calculated automatically.
  • Receipt: Optional upload of supporting document (PDF/JPG/PNG).
  • Staff Fields: Select who prepared, authorised, and collected the payment.
