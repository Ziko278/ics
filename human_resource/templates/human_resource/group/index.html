What are Permission Groups?
Groups (or Roles) are used to control what different types of users can see and do in the application.

After creating a group (e.g., "Teachers"), you must click the "Permissions" button to assign specific permissions to it, like the ability to view student records or enter grades.

Crucial Step: Once you've set up a group and its permissions, you can assign staff members to that group from the staff creation/editing page.
